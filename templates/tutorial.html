Welcome to DJ Mashup Generator


Follow this tutorial to learn how to choose a song from DJ Song Generator

  1. On the main page, choose a Category from the available options. This describes a category that describes the song type, like "Pop" or "Hip Hop".
    • Try choosing "Dance"
  2. If desired, choose a tempo as well. This is a musical characteristic describing how fast the song is.
    • Try choosing "120"
  3. You can also choose a key if you want! This is another musical characteristic describing the pitches in the song.
    • Try choosing "C"
  4. Click the "Generate a song choice!" button to get a suggested song matching the characteristics you selected.
